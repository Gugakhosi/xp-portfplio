About Guga

Hi, I'm Guga.

I'm a field computer technician who enjoys solving real-world tech problems. I work with computers, Windows systems, POS devices, printers, networking, hardware diagnostics, and user support.

I like technology that feels practical, useful, and reliable. Whether it's fixing a broken system, setting up equipment, troubleshooting Windows, or building a clean digital project, I enjoy finding the problem and making things work.

I'm also learning and building projects in app development, UI design, automation, and AI tools. This portfolio is part of that journey: a mix of my technical background, creativity, and love for old-school Windows XP style.

My goal is simple: keep improving, build better projects, and grow deeper into the tech world.
