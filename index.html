Flutter · Android · Personal Finance

Flutter Dart Mobile SQLite Active Dev

About this project

CachCash is my personal spending tracker built with Flutter. It's designed to make everyday money management painless: log every expense in seconds, group it by category, and watch monthly budgets to stay in control.

The app runs fully offline with a local SQLite database, so your data stays on your phone. I'm actively adding features — charts, recurring bills, and export to CSV are on the roadmap.

Key features

  • Quick add — log an expense in under 3 taps
  • Categories — food, transport, bills, leisure, and custom ones
  • Monthly budgets — set a cap, get warned before you go over
  • Stats — weekly trend chart + category breakdown
  • Offline-first — no account, no internet needed
  • Clean UI — designed to feel fast and native
